## v1.0.0

### Added

- Basic classes
- Events listeners (`CreateExchangeBind`, `RemoveExchangeBind`)
- Rabbit queue-based jobs failer
- A little bit extended commands `make:job` and `queue:work`
- `PrioritizedJobInterface` for prioritized jobs
